How We Make It

Crafted Fresh, Every Morning

01
Farm-Fresh Milk
We collect full-fat milk daily from partner dairies within 200km of our facility.
02
Natural Infusion
Real saffron strands, whole fruits, and roasted nuts are steeped overnight for depth.
03
Slow Churning
Our churners run at low speed to create the dense, creamy texture Mithas is known for.
04
Flash Frozen
Sealed and frozen within minutes to lock in freshness before reaching your home.
