Mithas started in a 200 sq ft kitchen in Mylapore. No fancy machinery, no investors — just one family's obsession with making ice cream taste like it actually came from the fruit, the nut, the spice.

"We didn't set out to build a brand. We just refused to accept that Indian ice cream had to taste artificial."
— Krishnah Iyengar, Founder

The journey

2012
Mylapore Kitchen
First batch of Kesar Pista sold at a local sabha canteen. Sold out in 40 minutes.
2015
First Storefront
Opened on Pondy Bazaar. Queue formed before the shutter went up on day one.
2018
Pan-India Delivery
Cracked cold-chain delivery to 40 cities. Alphonso Mango crossed 10,000 orders.
2022
50 Flavours
Launched the Royal Edition — Gulab Shahi, Zafrani Badam, and Thandai.
Today
Still Independent
No private equity. No artificial shortcuts. Same family. Same obsession.
🥛
Real Milk, Always
Full-fat milk sourced within 200 km of our facility, collected every morning before 6 AM. We've never used reconstituted milk powder.
🌿
Nothing Artificial
No artificial colours. No synthetic flavour compounds. If a fruit isn't in season, that flavour doesn't exist on our menu.
🤝
Farmer First
We pay above MSP to all 28 partner farms. Every mango, pistachio, and saffron thread comes with a face and a name behind it.

How We Make It

Crafted Fresh, Every Morning

01
Farm-Fresh Milk
We collect full-fat milk daily from partner dairies within 200km of our facility.
02
Natural Infusion
Real saffron strands, whole fruits, and roasted nuts are steeped overnight for depth.
03
Slow Churning
Our churners run at low speed to create the dense, creamy texture Mithas is known for.
04
Flash Frozen
Sealed and frozen within minutes to lock in freshness before reaching your home.
